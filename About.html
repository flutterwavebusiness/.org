Welcome to Flutterwave!

Flutterwave is an African fintech company that provides payment solutions for businesses and individuals.

The company is headquartered in San Francisco, USA, with a strong presence across Africa. As of 2026, Flutterwave is 10 years old, offering nearly a decade of experience in digital payments. It helps businesses accept payments, manage transactions, and expand globally with secure, multi-currency solutions.

Founded by Olugbenga Agboola and Iyinoluwa Aboyeji, Flutterwave aims to make African borders matter less by enabling fast, easy, and secure cross-border payments. With over 10 years of experience, Flutterwave has built a strong reputation across the continent.
